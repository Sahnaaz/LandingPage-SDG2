Sustainable Development Goal 3 - End Hunger

Every day, around 3,000 children die due to hunger-related causes, including malnutrition.

Globally, over 828 million people go to bed hungry each night, and 45 million people are at risk of famine in 43 countries.

Developed countries waste approximately 1.3 billion tons of food annually, enough to feed 820 million hungry people around the world.
